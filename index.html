THE COVID PANDEMIC

COVID-19 is a disease caused by SARS-CoV-2. The virus spreads by respiratory droplets and touching contaminated surfaces then touching your face.

Some symptoms include:

  • Coughing
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Fatigue
  • Muscle Aches
  • New Loss of Taste or Smell

Statistics:

  • There are over 152 million cases worldwide and 3.18 million deaths
  • 32.4 million cases and 576 thousand deaths from the United States
  • 1.23 million cases and 23.9 thousand deaths from Los Angeles County
